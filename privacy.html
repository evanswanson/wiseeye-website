Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2024

WiseEye.ai is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension and website handle your information in compliance with Google Chrome Web Store requirements.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from users. The WiseEye.ai Chrome extension operates locally in your browser and does not transmit your personal data, browsing history, or eBay credentials to our servers or any third party.

  • No account or sign-in is required to use the extension.
  • No cookies or tracking scripts are used by the extension.
  • We do not collect or store your eBay data.

2. Data Usage

All processing and negotiation logic happens locally in your browser. The extension only analyzes the content of eBay offer pages to provide negotiation assistance. No data is sent to WiseEye.ai or any external server.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties. Since we do not collect any personal or sensitive information, there is nothing to share.

4. User Controls

You are always in control. You can disable or remove the WiseEye.ai extension at any time through your browser's extension settings. No data is retained after removal.
